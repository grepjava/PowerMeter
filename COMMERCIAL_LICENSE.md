## Commercial Use — When a Commercial Licence is Required

A commercial licence is required if you or your organisation:

| Situation | Example |
|---|---|
| Bundle or redistribute PowerMeter as part of a **paid product or service** | Including PowerMeter (or a derivative) in a trading platform, indicator package, or SaaS offering sold to end users |
| Use PowerMeter inside a **proprietary trading firm or hedge fund** as part of a revenue-generating trading operation | Prop firm running PowerMeter on funded trader desks |
| Offer PowerMeter (or a derivative) as a **subscription or licence** to third parties | Charging traders a monthly fee to access a hosted or distributed version |
| Provide **consulting, training, or support services** built around PowerMeter where PowerMeter is a material component of the commercial offering | Selling a "Sierra Chart setup" package that includes a customised PowerMeter build |

If you are unsure whether your use requires a commercial licence, assume it does and reach out — most cases can be resolved quickly.

## Royalty Structure

Commercial licences are negotiated on a case-by-case basis. Typical structures:

- **Per-seat / per-user** — a one-time or annual fee per trader or workstation.
- **Revenue share** — a percentage of revenue attributable to products or services incorporating PowerMeter.
- **Flat annual licence** — a fixed annual fee appropriate for smaller operations.

There is no standard rate card — terms depend on the scale and nature of the use.
